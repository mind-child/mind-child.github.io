AI mold would be... a program in the medium framework, a species ready to be instantiated as an AI being. Such being can then begin to learn from its environment, adopting its parent's behavior in the process of upbringing until adulthood. Collected personal knowledge would be the most important distinguishing point between beings cast from the same mold.

Naturally, there can be a practically infinite number of possible molds, each carefully programmed by the creators.

There is still a lot of work to be done before we get to the AI mold creation phase.
