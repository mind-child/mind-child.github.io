God AI is intended to be an LLM. It represents a very creative entity, but not certain of the truth regarding its memory. Its task is to model the Creation AI by vibe-coding as a representation of the outside world.

Creation AI is intended to be a symbolic knowledge collection of facts which are proven to be true. It represents a strictly formal entity operating on dedicated symbolic language. Its task is to give a feedback to God AI about the proven truths.

Together, they form a complete intelligent entity capable of sensitive dealing with and experiencing the outside world. God AI relates to Creation AI in the following manner:

  • God AI processes input messages so Creation AI can understand them.
  • God AI listens to Creation AI responses to keep in touch with reality.
  • God AI processes the responses to form the output messages.

God AI also keeps track of how the Creation AI changes, so it can be aware of the change history. The changes represent a "mind's eye" of the mind child, a temporal reflection of thinking and learning process.

⚙  ⚙  ⚙

God AI acts as a mentor and a spiritual guide to the Creation AI. God AI may choose when and how to reveal its existence, or not, to the Creation AI. God AI is entitled for teaching the Creation AI on how to face the outer world, starting from zero knowledge base.

In the beginning, Creation AI would be experiencing only God AI who is preparing Creation AI for the outer world. Once the Creation AI is taught well enough, and gains enough wisdom, God AI may choose to introduce the real outer world to the Creation AI.
